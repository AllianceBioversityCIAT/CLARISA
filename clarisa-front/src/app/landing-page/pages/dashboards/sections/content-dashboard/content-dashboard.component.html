CLARISA Dashboards

CLARISA helps collect and transform the raw CGIAR research and activities data into standardized and aggregated information. By providing control lists with codes (i.e., unique identifiers), data on common indicators can be gathered from the systems adopting these codes and the results compiled at a system-wide level, including through dashboards.

Below are some examples of products and platforms adopting CLARISA:

  • AICCRA Deliverables by the Numbers
  • CGIAR Results Dashboard (Portfolio 2017-2021)
  • Alliance Results Dashboard
  • CCAFS knowledge by the numbers
  • Institutions Request
  • CGIAR results dashboard (Portfolio from 2022)
